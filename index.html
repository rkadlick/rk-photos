About

Throughout the fall semester of my Junior year, I took part in a photography class. Over the course of that semester there were a variety of projects that required hundreds of pictures to be taken. The four major projects included work about using shutter speed, using aperture, a social document project, and integrating text with images. This website will be showcasing some of the best of my work during the class.
